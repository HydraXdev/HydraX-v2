PRIVACY POLICY

Effective Date: January 1, 2024 | Last Updated: January 1, 2024

Your Privacy Matters: BITTEN is committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Telegram username, email address (if provided)
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through Stripe (we don't store card details)
  • Profile Data: Learning preferences, tier selection, notification settings
  • Communications: Support tickets, feedback, correspondence

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: Signal views, feature usage, login times
  • Performance Data: XP earned, achievements, learning statistics
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information
  • Cookies: Session management and preference storage

2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the BITTEN educational service
  • Send signal notifications and market alerts
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions
  • Track learning progress and calculate XP
  • Improve our services and develop new features
  • Communicate updates and respond to support requests
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. DATA SHARING AND DISCLOSURE

We do NOT sell your personal information. We may share data with:

3.1 Service Providers

  • Stripe: Payment processing
  • Telegram: Message delivery
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics
  • Hosting: Secure data storage

3.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government request.

4. DATA SECURITY

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access to personal information
  • Secure payment processing through Stripe
  • Two-factor authentication options

5. YOUR RIGHTS AND CHOICES

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correct: Update inaccurate information
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Object: Opt-out of certain data uses
  • Portability: Export your data in a readable format
  • Withdraw Consent: Cancel your subscription anytime

6. DATA RETENTION

We retain your data as follows:

Data Type Retention Period
Account Information Until account deletion + 30 days
Learning Performance Duration of subscription + 90 days
Payment Records 7 years (legal requirement)
Support Communications 2 years

7. COOKIES AND TRACKING

We use cookies for:

  • Session management and authentication
  • Remembering user preferences
  • Analytics (anonymous usage data)
  • Security and fraud prevention

You can control cookies through your browser settings.

8. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

BITTEN is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. INTERNATIONAL TRANSFERS

Your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States. By using BITTEN, you consent to this transfer.

10. CALIFORNIA PRIVACY RIGHTS

California residents have additional rights under CCPA including:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of data sales (we don't sell data)
  • Right to non-discrimination

11. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We'll notify you of material changes via email or service notification.
