REFUND POLICY

Last Updated: January 2024

15-DAY FREE TRIAL: All new users receive a 15-day free trial with full access to features. No payment required during trial period.

1. GENERAL REFUND POLICY

BITTEN operates on a monthly subscription basis. Due to the nature of our digital service and immediate access to trading signals, we have a limited refund policy.

2. ELIGIBILITY FOR REFUNDS

Refunds may be issued in the following circumstances:

  • Technical Issues: Service unavailable for more than 72 consecutive hours due to our system failures
  • Billing Errors: Duplicate charges or incorrect billing amounts
  • First 7 Days: New paying customers may request a refund within 7 days of first payment if unsatisfied

3. NON-REFUNDABLE SITUATIONS

Refunds will NOT be issued for:

  • Trading losses or poor performance
  • Market conditions or volatility
  • User error or misconfiguration
  • Partial month usage
  • Violation of Terms of Service resulting in account termination
  • Change of mind after 7-day period

4. CANCELLATION POLICY

  • You may cancel your subscription at any time
  • Cancellation takes effect at the end of current billing period
  • You retain access until the end of your paid period
  • No partial refunds for unused time

5. HOW TO REQUEST A REFUND

To request a refund:

  1. Contact support within the eligible timeframe
  2. Provide your Telegram username and transaction details
  3. Explain the reason for your refund request
  4. Allow 5-10 business days for processing

6. REFUND PROCESSING

  • Approved refunds are processed through Stripe
  • Funds typically appear in 5-10 business days
  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method
  • You will receive confirmation once processed

7. TIER CHANGES AND PRORATION

  • Upgrades: Charged immediately with proration
  • Downgrades: Take effect at next billing cycle
  • No refunds for tier downgrades

8. FREE TRIAL TERMS

  • 15-day trial includes full access to all features
  • No payment information required during trial
  • Trial automatically expires after 15 days
  • One trial per user lifetime

9. DISPUTE RESOLUTION

If you're unsatisfied with our refund decision:

  • Contact our support team for escalation
  • Provide additional documentation if needed
  • Allow 48 hours for management review
  • Final decisions are at our sole discretion

10. PAYMENT FAILURES

  • Failed payments enter a 2-day grace period
  • Access continues during grace period
  • After grace period, features are locked
  • No refunds for failed payment periods

11. CONTACT SUPPORT

For refund requests or questions:

  • Telegram Support: @YourSupportBot
  • Response time: Within 24 hours
  • Please include transaction ID and date

NOTE: This refund policy is subject to change. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Your continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the modified policy.
